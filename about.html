Jo-Jean Imoh-Ita - FOUNDER

With over a decade of experience in personal development and executive coaching, Jo-Jean Imoh-Ita has empowered hundreds of individuals to achieve their goals and live more fulfilling lives. Her holistic approach combines practical strategies with deep personal insight to create lasting transformation.

Jo-Jean is a certified Life Coach and holds advanced certifications in Leadership Development and Career Coaching. Her background in organizational psychology and human behavior gives her unique insight into helping clients overcome obstacles and achieve breakthrough results.

Based in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Jo-Jean works with clients both locally and internationally through virtual coaching sessions. Her mission is to help every person discover their unique strengths, clarify their vision, and take confident action toward the life they truly want to live.
